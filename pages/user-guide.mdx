# User Guide

## How it Works

1. Download the YKYR extension
2. Create account
3. Turn on and collect data
4. Earn rewards

## Reward Systems

Users participating in the YKYR ecosystem are rewarded for their contributions in several ways:

* **Data Contribution Rewards**: Miners (data contributors) earn rewards based on the quality and quantity of the browsing data they provide.
* **Testnet Incentives**: During testnet phases, users can earn bonus rewards for active participation and feedback, helping to improve the platform before the mainnet launch.
* **Sell data**: Opportunity to monetize personal data through the platform.
